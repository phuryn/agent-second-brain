### HYP-002 — Bacterial pre-training improves human membrane protein prediction
**Was:** Pre-training on bacterial protein structures improves prediction accuracy for human membrane proteins.
**Evidence against:** 4 experiments, no improvement. Bacterial membrane topology differs too much — transfer adds noise.
**Key learning:** Cross-kingdom transfer learning fails for membrane proteins. Within-kingdom transfer works. Test soluble proteins separately before assuming the same.
**Rejected:** 2026-02-28
